Dr Ashish Kumar Meena
MBBS

Hi, I am Dr Ashish Kumar Meena, a doctor by profession .I have completed my MBBS and currently pursuing my MD degree in the field of Community Medicine also known Preventive and Social medicine (PSM) from GMC, Bhopal . Before starting PG in PSM I served in AIIMS Bhopal as a junior resident doctor for a year in Paediatrics dept. followed by about 14 months of service in Red cross Hospital Bhopal where I was a CMO and dealt with emergencies and routine OPD/IPD patients.

  • 01THE HARSH REALITY

    One of the toughest times of our era, the COVID disease came to our test , which we so far have fought whole heartedly with all our might . After having done around 800-1000 hours duty in COVID , dealing from asymptomatic to critically ill patients from general wards to ICUs , I realized it was a real painful affair for people from all walks of life . We saved many lives but lost quite a few too. The joy of saving a life comes down to nothing in a flash when you lose a patient the very next moment.

  • 02 FIRST AID

    My first big experience of dealing with emergencies came at Red Cross hospital where at times I was the only attending doctor and had to deal with everything starting from minor wounds to suturing, stomach ache to heart attacks, minor UTIs to renal stone pain!!

  • 03 BORN AN ATHLETE

    Since my early school days I have been attracted more towards sports as compared to studies. From being an active participant in football and cricket games, I represented my school athletic team in inter-state athletic clusters in track events twice and won medals though in relays only!! My sports journey continued through cricket in college days. This is when I started observing the difference in attitude, physique, intelligence and approach to problems of the athletes as compared to non-athletes.

  • 04THE MOTIVATION

    The seed for it was sown much earlier, when I had a mixed interest in sports and my nurturing will to become a doctor. Both the things though are much apart since one requires a lot of dedicated study time and the other requires to straining yourself outdoors but fitness was one thing that came out as a common denominator for both the equations in my case. As sports are basically making yourself fit while a doctor strives to make others fit.

  • 05 GIVE AWAY

    Finally with all the experiences and whatsoever progress I have made in the respective fields along with experiences and expertise of my other team members of nutritionists and and fitness enthusiasts ,we have decided to form a regime of diet and exercises with meal plans and counselling in the most natural way possible for the benefit of people .
